About Our Masjid

Islamic Society of Sheboygan is a vibrant community center providing religious, educational, and social services to Muslims in Sheboygan and surrounding areas.

Our mission is to serve the religious needs of Muslims in our community and promote understanding and cooperation between Muslims and people of other faiths.
