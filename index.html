Welcome to Sheboygan!

Our mosque is proud to serve Sheboygan County and surrounding areas! Everyone is welcome! If you have any questions please feel free to contact us in the contact section below.

Prayer Times

We have 5 daily prayers at the masjid conducted by the Imam.

Friday prayer (Jumu'ah): 1:10 PM

Quran Jalsa

We hold a weekly Quran Jalsa every Saturday after Maghrib to dive deep into different Ayas from the Quran. Everyone is welcome!

For first time visitors, please feel free to message us in the contact section below to guarantee no cancellations for this week.

Sunday School

Quran classes are held for children of all ages and adults. Classes start at 11 am and go until 2 pm every Sunday. We then end with Duhr prayer.

Our Sunday school is currently on summer break. Please contact us below for most up to date info!
