Islamic Studies

Quran Jalsa

We hold a weekly Quran Jalsa every Saturday after Maghrib to dive deep into different Ayas from the Quran. Everyone is welcome!

For first time visitors, please feel free to message us in the contact section below to guarantee no cancellations for this week.

Sunday School

Quran classes are held for children of all ages and adults. Classes start at 9:30 am every Sunday. We then end with Duhr prayer.
